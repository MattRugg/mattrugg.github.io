Boutique Electronics Development

We craft innovative electronic solutions tailored to your vision. From concept to prototype to production, our expert team delivers cutting-edge hardware and firmware development for businesses ready to bring their ideas to life.

Our Core Capabilities

Discover the breadth of our expertise, from analog and digital design to IoT, RF, compliance, and embedded security. We deliver innovative, reliable solutions for every stage of your electronics project.

Mixed-Signal Design

Complete analog and digital circuit solutions from precision op-amps and signal conditioning to microcontroller systems and ADC/DAC interfaces.

SoM Integration

Seamlessly integrate existing System-on-Modules (SoMs) into custom designs, bridging computing platforms with specialized peripherals and interfaces.

Full-Stack IoT Solutions

Complete end-to-end IoT ecosystems with robust backend services, cloud infrastructure, and intuitive frontends for both web platforms and embedded device interfaces.

Compliance Support

End-to-end support through regulatory compliance, ensuring your products meet industry standards and certification requirements.

RF Solutions

Wireless connectivity solutions including Bluetooth Low Energy, WiFi, and custom ISM band implementations for IoT applications.

Embedded Security

Secure-by-design embedded systems and comprehensive IoT security audits. We build safe devices from the ground up and evaluate existing systems for vulnerabilities.
